Lasagna

Description

This recipe will yield a hearty and simple lasagna that can be enjoyed as a dinnertime main dish as well as a leftover snack. DISCLAIMER: this recipe is not real and should not be attempted!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish.
  3. Let bake for 30 minutes.
  4. Let cool and serve!
