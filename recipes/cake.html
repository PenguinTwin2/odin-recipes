Cake

Description

This is a step-by-step recipe on baking a "cake".

Ingredients

  • Brass
  • Percussion
  • Color guard
  • Drum majors
  • Optional: a singular woodwind, guitar(s), prop(s)

Steps

  1. Have all the "ingredients" audition and pay tour fees.
  2. Spend a few weeks in a stadium of your choice for spring training.
  3. Tour around the United States and compete in DCI competitions.
  4. With enough time and money, you're on your way to having a World Champion Cake!
