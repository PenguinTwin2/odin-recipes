Instant Ramen

Description

This quick and easy recipe will guide you on the fulfilling journey that is making instant ramen. Recommended for college students or anyone too busy or unmotivated to make a proper meal.

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen
  • Boiling water

Steps

  1. Open instant ramen container halfway; don't remove lid completely.
  2. Pour water into container and cover with lid.
  3. Wait 3 minutes or when noodles are at desired softness.
  4. Remove lid and enjoy!
